Databases

Here is a list of some Databases that I think useful for who want to learn about single cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) and related information and tools.

Field Database Description Related paper
Single-cell scRNASeqDB Human Single Cell Transcriptome Database
Single-cell Azimuth App for reference based single-cell analysis paper
Single-cell CancerSCEM Cancer Single-cell Expression Map paper (PMID: 34643725)
Single-cell Curated cancer cell atlas (3CA) Collected, annotated and analyzed cancer scRNA-seq datasets paper
GWAS GWAS Catalog The NHGRI-EBI Catalog of human genome-wide association studies
GWAS UK Biobank The world’s most important health research database
GWAS ieu_MR-base MR-base is a database and analytical platform for Mendelian randomization
GWAS ezQTL ezQTL is a web-based tool for integrative QTL (Quantitative Trait Loci) visualization and colocalization with GWAS data for individual loci to aid GWAS annotation.
GWAS GWAS Summary Statistics GWAS Summary Statistics for Brain Imaging Phenotypes paper
GWAS SSGAC Data Portal The NHGRI-EBI Catalog of human genome-wide association studies paper
Brain CHCP Chinese Human Connectome Project paper
